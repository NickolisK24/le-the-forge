# v4.5B.5 Deterministic Evidence Panels

## Architectural Purpose

v4.5B.5 establishes deterministic governance-safe public evidence panel
infrastructure on top of the v4.5A governance-safe drift intelligence chain,
v4.5B.1 trust visibility foundations, v4.5B.2 support status visibility,
v4.5B.3 explainability surfaces, and v4.5B.4 provenance and lineage
visibility.

The phase makes evidence panels visible for trust summaries, support statuses,
explainability surfaces, provenance visibility, lineage visibility,
unsupported-state visibility, limitation explanations, and public diagnostics.
It remains descriptive-only public evidence infrastructure.

## Deterministic Evidence Panel Philosophy

Evidence panels describe evidence references, groupings, source visibility,
freshness visibility, continuity visibility, provenance visibility, lineage
visibility, unsupported evidence, missing evidence, and evidence-panel
diagnostics.

Evidence panels do not create correctness guarantees, evidence authority, trust
scores, approval semantics, authorization semantics, operational readiness,
execution safety, production enablement, recommendation priority, or ranking
priority.

## Evidence Grouping Philosophy

Evidence grouping visibility deterministically describes trust summary evidence,
support status evidence, explainability evidence, provenance evidence, lineage
evidence, limitation evidence, unsupported-state evidence, and diagnostics
evidence.

Grouping is visibility only. It does not prioritize, rank, recommend, authorize,
approve, or score evidence.

## Evidence Source Visibility Philosophy

Evidence source visibility exposes deterministic source references, evidence
origin references, evidence provenance references, evidence lineage references,
evidence continuity references, and evidence diagnostics references.

Source visibility remains descriptive-only. It does not grant source authority,
approval semantics, authorization semantics, scoring semantics, or operational
meaning.

## Evidence Freshness Visibility Philosophy

Evidence freshness visibility exposes current evidence, stale evidence, unknown
freshness evidence, incomplete freshness evidence, and unsupported freshness
evidence.

Freshness visibility remains explicit and fail-visible. It does not trigger
automatic refresh, fallback substitution, remediation, repair, mitigation,
authorization, approval, ranking, recommendation, scoring, or runtime behavior.

## Support Status Evidence Panel Philosophy

Support status evidence panels describe evidence for supported, partially
supported, unsupported, experimental, deprecated, blocked, and unknown support
states.

Support status evidence panels do not approve, authorize, rank, recommend,
score, execute, or imply production readiness.

## Explainability Evidence Panel Philosophy

Explainability evidence panels describe support explanation evidence,
limitation explanation evidence, unsupported-state explanation evidence,
continuity explanation evidence, trust explanation evidence, and diagnostic
explanation evidence.

Explainability evidence panels remain descriptive-only and do not recommend,
rank, authorize, approve, or create operational semantics.

## Provenance and Lineage Evidence Panel Philosophy

Provenance and lineage evidence panels describe provenance evidence, lineage
evidence, source-to-surface evidence, evidence origin panels, stale or unknown
provenance evidence, and incomplete lineage evidence.

These panels preserve provenance and lineage visibility without creating trust
scores, source scores, ranking, recommendation, authorization, approval, or
runtime traversal behavior.

## Unsupported and Missing Evidence Philosophy

Missing support evidence, missing explanation evidence, missing provenance
evidence, missing lineage evidence, unsupported evidence states, incomplete
evidence states, blocked evidence states, and unknown evidence states remain
explicit and fail-visible.

No unsupported or missing evidence state is suppressed, silently replaced,
backfilled, scored, or converted into operational behavior.

## Fail-Visible Evidence Panel Diagnostics Philosophy

Fail-visible diagnostics expose missing evidence panel references, incomplete
evidence grouping, missing evidence source visibility, stale evidence
visibility, unknown freshness visibility, unsupported evidence panel states,
source-to-panel continuity gaps, inherited evidence limitation gaps, and
inherited evidence prohibition gaps.

Diagnostics do not remediate, repair, mitigate, correct, approve, authorize,
rank, recommend, score, route, traverse, dispatch, schedule, sequence, execute,
or mutate runtime state.

## Hashing Guarantees

v4.5B.5 provides deterministic SHA-256 hashing for evidence panel records,
evidence group records, evidence item records, evidence source visibility
records, evidence freshness visibility records, support status evidence panel
records, explainability evidence panel records, provenance and lineage evidence
panel records, evidence panel diagnostics, and the full exported evidence

Hashes are stable, deterministic, replay-safe, provenance-safe, lineage-safe,
and integrity-safe.
