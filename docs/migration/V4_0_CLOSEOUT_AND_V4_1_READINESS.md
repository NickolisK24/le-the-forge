# v4.0 Closeout And v4.1 Readiness

## Architectural Purpose

v4.0 Phase 10 closes out the deterministic operational lifecycle governance stack and certifies v4.1 planning readiness. It verifies that v4.0 established deterministic operational lifecycle intelligence without introducing execution, orchestration, production consumption, recommendation systems, ranking, scoring, selection, remediation, mutation, planner integration, approval, or authorization behavior.

The phase performs deterministic closeout auditing and v4.1 readiness certification. It does not enable operational execution, authorize production consumption, remediate blockers, or approve operational behavior.

## Closeout Philosophy

Closeout certification means deterministic proof that the v4.0 governance chain remained operationally safe. v4.1 readiness means governance infrastructure is stable enough for future planning only.

Closeout and readiness statuses remain descriptive evidence. They do not imply execution readiness, production activation, deployment approval, remediation authority, production consumption, orchestration, planner integration, or operational workflow enablement.

## Hard Prohibitions

Phase 10 does not introduce execution behavior, orchestration behavior, production consumption, production activation, deployment behavior, refresh execution, patch execution, rollback execution, recovery execution, recommendation behavior, ranking behavior, scoring behavior, selection behavior, optimization behavior, remediation behavior, repair behavior, automatic correction, approval behavior, authorization behavior, scheduling, routing, dispatch, runtime mutation, hidden fallback behavior, automatic blocker resolution, planner integration behavior, or callable operational workflows.

## Deterministic Closeout Guarantees

Closeout auditing uses deterministic dataclass-style models, stable finding types, stable deterministic keys, and explicit source references. Each finding preserves source phase, lifecycle reference, drift reference, governance reference, validation reference, production-consumption reference, recovery reference, diagnostics reference, integrity reference, continuity reference, provenance reference, lineage reference, replay reference, rollback reference, explanation, severity, and deterministic key.

The closeout report preserves deterministic closeout status, v4.1 readiness status, finding counts, visibility counts, preservation booleans, authorization flags, production-consumption flags, orchestration flags, and planner-integration flags.

## Deterministic Serialization Guarantees

Serialization preserves deterministic finding ordering and all closeout evidence. Warning, prohibited, unknown, non-execution, non-remediation, non-authorization, production-consumption-disabled, orchestration-disabled, and planner-integration-disabled findings are not omitted.

Serialization does not hide warnings, escalate readiness, authorize behavior, repair blockers, or convert descriptive closeout evidence into operational capability.

## Deterministic Hashing Guarantees

Closeout hashing includes lifecycle identity, drift/governance/validation hashes, production consumption/recovery/diagnostics/integrity/continuity hashes, closeout status, v4.1 readiness status, deterministic finding keys, severity counts, preservation booleans, authorization flags, orchestration flags, and planner-integration flags.

Repeated closeout report generation produces stable hashes for unchanged evidence.

## Fail-Visible Warning Behavior

Warning visibility remains explicit in the closeout report. Warnings do not authorize execution, remediation, production consumption, orchestration, planner integration, ranking, scoring, selection, or optimization.

## Prohibited, Unknown, And Warning Visibility

Prohibited, unknown, and warning evidence remains visible as closeout findings. These findings preserve operational limitations and do not convert uncertainty into readiness, authorization, remediation, or production activation.

## Replay And Rollback Safety

Replay and rollback safety are preserved from the v4.0 evidence chain. The closeout layer does not execute replay, rollback, recovery, refresh, deployment, or patch behavior.

## Provenance And Lineage Safety

Provenance and lineage references remain connected across lifecycle, drift, governance, validation, production consumption, recovery, diagnostics, integrity, and continuity evidence. The closeout layer does not infer missing provenance, repair lineage gaps, or mutate source reports.

## Serialization, Hash, And Visibility Preservation

Serialization stability, hashing stability, and visibility preservation are certified as deterministic evidence properties. They remain descriptive closeout evidence only.

## Non-Execution Preservation

Execution remains unauthorized and non-executable across closeout evidence.

## Non-Remediation Preservation

Remediation remains unauthorized. Closeout certification does not remediate, repair, correct, migrate, or resolve blockers.

## Production-Consumption-Disabled Preservation

Production consumption remains disabled. Closeout certification does not consume trusted bundles, load bundles into planners, activate production behavior, or change planner behavior.

## Orchestration-Disabled Preservation

Orchestration remains disabled. Closeout certification does not schedule, route, dispatch, execute, or create callable operational workflows.

## Planner-Integration-Disabled Preservation

Planner integration remains disabled. Closeout certification does not alter planner data sources, planner behavior, or production planner integration.

## Explicit Execution Statement

Execution remains unauthorized.

## Explicit Production Consumption Statement

Production consumption remains disabled.

## Explicit Orchestration Statement

Orchestration remains disabled.
