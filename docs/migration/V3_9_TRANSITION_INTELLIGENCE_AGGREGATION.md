## Aggregation Is Not Orchestration

Aggregation combines evidence records into deterministic summaries. It does not execute transitions, authorize transitions, traverse graphs, choose routes, schedule dispatch, approve behavior, mutate runtime state, or produce production pathways.

## Visibility Aggregation

Visibility aggregation is descriptive only. It records fail-visible findings, unsupported states, prohibited states, uncertainty, continuity, provenance, explainability, integrity, governance, and scenario risk visibility.

## Fail-Visible Findings

No aggregation state or visibility record is hidden, silently corrected, prioritized, weighted, recommended, ranked, scored, or selected.

## Builds On Previous v3.9 Layers
