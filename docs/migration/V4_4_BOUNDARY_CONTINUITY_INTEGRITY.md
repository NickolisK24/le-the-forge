# v4.4 Boundary Continuity and Integrity Certification

## Continuity Certification Architecture

v4.4 Phase 7 certifies deterministic continuity visibility across the completed v4.4 boundary intelligence chain:

- Phase 1 boundary foundations
- Phase 2 inheritance and refinement intelligence
- Phase 3 conflict and drift intelligence
- Phase 4 cross-boundary consistency intelligence
- Phase 5 segmentation and scope intelligence
- Phase 6 explainability and diagnostic aggregation

Continuity certification is descriptive evidence only. It does not authorize, approve, recommend, decide, activate, route, dispatch, schedule, execute, or mutate orchestration behavior.

No certification system grants runtime authority.

## Integrity Certification Architecture

Integrity certification records make phase evidence integrity, source reference integrity, serialization integrity, hashing integrity, diagnostic integrity, explainability integrity, provenance integrity, lineage integrity, limitation integrity, and summary integrity visible.

Integrity status is not a production-consumption signal and is not an approval signal.

No integrity result authorizes orchestration behavior.

## Phase Evidence Reference Guarantees

Phase evidence references identify the six v4.4 boundary intelligence phases and their generated report/hash references. References are deterministic pointers only.

Phase evidence references do not consume production data, activate runtime behavior, infer readiness, or connect planners.

## Phase-Chain Continuity Guarantees

The phase-chain identity preserves the ordered v4.4 boundary intelligence sequence. Continuous, discontinuous, replay-safe, rollback-safe, provenance-safe, and lineage-safe states remain deterministic and fail-visible.

Continuity visibility does not repair gaps and does not activate runtime behavior.

## Integrity Visibility Guarantees

Integrity visibility covers:

- phase evidence integrity

- deterministic serialization integrity
- deterministic hashing integrity
- diagnostic integrity

Integrity warnings and integrity-blocked findings remain visible. They are diagnostic evidence, not approvals or production readiness signals.

## Certification Limitation Guarantees

## Replay and Rollback Safety Guarantees

Replay and rollback evidence references are deterministic and tied to the same phase evidence identifiers. Replay-safe and rollback-safe classifications are evidence visibility only.

Replay and rollback safety do not unlock execution, planner integration, production consumption, or runtime mutation.
