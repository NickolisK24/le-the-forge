# v4.1 Closeout and v4.2 Readiness

Phase 10 closes out v4.1 and certifies v4.2 planning readiness. The closeout
layer audits the Phase 1-9 refresh governance evidence, report coverage,
integrity coverage, continuity coverage, replay and rollback safety,
provenance and lineage safety, warning visibility, and prohibited-capability
boundaries.

This phase is closeout certification infrastructure only. It does not
operationalize refresh systems and it does not authorize production behavior.

## Scope

- v4.1 closeout identity.
- v4.1 phase coverage certification.
- v4.1 report coverage certification.
- v4.1 integrity verification.
- v4.1 continuity verification.
- v4.1 governance verification.
- v4.1 replay and rollback safety verification.
- v4.1 provenance and lineage verification.
- Fail-visible warning aggregation.
- Unsupported, prohibited, and blocked state aggregation.
- Deterministic v4.2 planning readiness visibility.
- Deterministic serialization, hashing, equality, integrity auditing, and
  closeout readiness certification.

intelligence only. v4.2 planning readiness is certified with warnings for
